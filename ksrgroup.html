  KSR GROUPS

  A Small steps towards literacy, made into a giant leap in education, Sixteen institutions, 400 acres, 30,000 students, 5000 faculty, Kintergarden to Ph.D. level, engineering, dental, science and humanities, management, polytechnic, industrial training, schools, all in one campus in Tiruchengode, Tamil Nadu. Lion Dr. K. S. Rangasamy MJF Founder-Chairman hails from rural background whose dream in uplifting rural into an educational hub offering quality education is made reality now. Eventually, with a wow to inspire and to fulfil the education needs of the rural youth, he started a polytechnic college in 1984 followed by three engineering colleges, two polytechnics, two arts and science colleges, a dental college, education college, industrial training institute, management studies and four schools. Currently, K.S.R. Educational Institutions (KSREI) is a township for multi-faceted and diversified education with 16 Institutions.

KSR Engineering colleges were established to promote the professionalism of the rural masses. Autonomous status helps to periodically review the course contents and mechanisms of course delivery in the light of changing and anticipated demands of the industries. College(s) are approved by competent authorities and 2 (f) and 12(B) status by UGC, New Delhi. Institutions are approved by NAAC and sponsored by DST-FIST. Colleges are life member in Institution of Engineers (INDIA), ISTE, IIPE, SAE, IWS, IETE, ICTACT &ISIOI. Partners Institution with IIT Alumni Centre for Pan IIT Alumni Leadership Series (PALS). Infrastructure has been created to conduct online courses through QEEE, NPTEL and NMEICT.

National Instruments Centre for Excellence (NICE) provides necessary expertise facilities and technical know-how for understanding R&D projects in emerging inter-disciplinary areas of relevance to industry. Parametric Technology Corporation (PTC) centre of excellence is established to impart modern technical skills and knowledge to create industry ready human capital and enhance employment opportunities for the students. Institutions are partner with M/s Infosys for the skill development through campus connect programme. SAP (ABAP, FI, MM & HR) and Oracle trainings are given to the students regularly. CCNA course is rolled out with the support of CISCO academy. Autodesk is supporting the Institutions to impart the design and analysis skills for core engineering students. The Institutes awarded the first place by Anna University for filing maximum number of patents in the year 2016. Apart from core and IT, research and career courses like Food Technology, Nanotechnology, Biotechnology and Mechatronics are offered. Curriculum and syllabi designed for Outcome-Based Education (OBE) and Choice-Based Credit System (CBCS). Usage of ICT tools like MOODLE, MOOC are facilitated for teaching and learning processes.

Career counseling is an intergral component of the educational mission of KSREI. It is committed to providing an extended curriculum to develop inquisitive, independent thinkers with a love for learning.

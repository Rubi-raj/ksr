  AGE RULES

  • 3 years as on 31st July of the academic year for admission to LKG. For other classes the age wilt be reckoned accordingly with 31st July as reference date.

  • For lower Kinder Garten (LKG) admission is granted if the child is physically and mentally in form.

  • For UKG and other classes uptoStd - IX attainment in the previous class is assessed by means of an entrance test and admission is granted on merit.

  • The following certificates are to be submitted at the time of admission.

  LKG, UKG &Std – 1

  • Date of Birth ( Birth Certificate)

  • Original and Xerox

  Std – II to IX

  • Transfer Certificate

  Std - XI

  • TC

  • X std Mark Statement

  • Community Certificate

  EMIS : ( Educational Management Information System)

  • If any student newly joined in our school they have to collect EMIS No – 16 digit no from Previous school and submit it to the office at the time of Admission.

  Payment of Fees

  • School Fees for the entire academic year has been divided into 3 Terms.

  • I Term – School Fees with Notebooks and Bus Fees has to be paid on or before 30th June 2016.

  • II Term – School fees and Bus Fees within October 10th 2016.

  • III Term – School Fees and Bus fees within January 10th 2017.
